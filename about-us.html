ABOUT US

About Game Hub

Game Hub was established in 2022 in the small town of Fredrikstad, south of Oslo. The vision for the company is to create a market-leading platform for buying and selling used games. We strongly believe in the development of product reuse in this market, and we strive to be competitive on price at all times.

Selection of games

Game Hub trades in and sells used games on PC and the most popular consoles. We aim to be the largest trading library of used games online. We can proudly say that we already have a large and growing selection of games for most devices.

Quality of games

To ensure game functionality and customer satisfaction, all games go through a fixed routine check before they are listed for sale on our website. This involves washing, cleaning and testing of cartridges and discs, as well as checking cover labels and cases. We still take account of normal wear and tear.

Good service

We at Game Hub take great pride in our customer service. We put the customer first, and we will therefore do our utmost to ensure that you as a customer experience both the purchase process and the products as satisfactory. We encourage you to contact our customer service for all appropriate enquiries. Thank you for visiting Game Hub.
